MUM & DAD


As parents we have a hard job to do, it is not an easy task but despite our own needs and desires we get on with it, we get our hands dirty and do what is needed for the best of our children. We do anything that is needed, cooking, washing, cleaning, bathing, changing, feeding, teaching and playing.  We are the childs cook, cleaner, nursemaid, servant, teacher, parent, friend  ... but hold on a minute..  

... WHAT ABOUT YOU!


Where do you come into this?  it can't all be about the kids.  No, you need your time out as well, call it ME TIME, if you get quality time to yourself then this can only reflect positively on the children.  

If you are struggling to get time out then please ask for help (link to supporting you), its here waiting for you, and there are all sorts of ways in which help can be provided, for yourself your child, or even financially.. if you dont ask, well, you know the rest.
