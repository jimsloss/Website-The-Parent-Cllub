Being a parent is an emotional rollercoaster, full of exciting highs and stressful lows.  Regardless of your age, fitness, intelligence or background, your mental, physical or financial stability.. it's tough, but consider this site as your tour guide, here to help you get more of the highs and steer out of the lows.



This is not a professional website offering targeted advice or run by a governing body, nor does it have any fancy advertising or promotion, it is simply an informal site created by parents, for parents.


Its objections are simple;

  • To pass on useful advice and information,
  • To promote the sharing of information between parents,
  • To provide a time out zone, where you can either chat with other parents or get something of your chest,
  • and to help you to enjoy being a parent just a little bit more .
